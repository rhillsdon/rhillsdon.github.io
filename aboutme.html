About Me

Who I am, what I love, and what I do.

I'm a 20 year old college student currently studying Game Development at Algonquin College. Games have been a passion of mine ever since I was able to play them, and when I got my hands on the first of them to come with a level editor, I was instantly enamored. Computers, Game Design, and Programming are my primary interests as well as my planned career path. Since my early childhood days, I've been dabbling in every engine that I could get my hands on, from Source back in the 2000s to Unreal. Eventually one thing led to another and I quickly found myself heading down the path of game/software development.

I've been carefully following the games industry since highschool, and making sure to stay abreast of any current issues, big titles, and important developments. However, aside from games I also find psychology, computer science and writing extremely interesting. Recently I've also begun to dabble with the systems in various tabletop games, and have started a fair bit of homebrew.

On this website, you will find a collection of my past and current works. If you have any questions or inquiries, feel free to drop me a line at richard.hillsdon@rogers.com.
